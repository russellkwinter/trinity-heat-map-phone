Canada Site Intelligence

A map of Canadian real-estate market data, from whole metro areas right down to individual neighbourhoods. Three tabs, three questions:

  • 🍁Metro Markets — compare whole metros on a score you control with the weighting sliders.
  • 🏠Neighbourhoods — zoom into any metro (or pick a focus city) for income, density and ownership block by block.
  • 🌐GTA Cities — Greater Toronto municipalities by income, diversity and more.

The “Now showing” line under the tabs always says, in plain words, what the colours mean. Brighter green = better, deeper red = worse.

Data & sources

Every metric, its source and vintage. Values are populated only where verified; gaps are left blank rather than estimated.

Metro markets · 41 CMAs
Population growth2016–2021 Census of Population change.
Crime severity2024 StatCan Crime Severity Index — weighted measure of police-reported crime. Oshawa excluded by StatCan (policing-boundary mismatch).
Rapid transitRail service tier (0–3): rail / LRT / commuter, scored against the present-day network.
Poverty rateCFLIM-AT, 2023 (StatCan T1FF table 11-10-0018) — after-tax low-income measure.
Median incomeMedian total income, 2023 (StatCan T1FF table 11-10-0008).
Property taxResidential rate — Ontario 2023, all other metros 2025 (WOWA / municipal sources). Quebec metros use city-wide aggregate residential rates (medium confidence).
Neighbourhoods · curated cities
Crime /100kCity police open data — Toronto (TPS, 2019), Calgary (CPS, 2024), Vancouver (VPD, 2024), Edmonton (EPS, 2025), Ottawa (OPS, 2025), Montréal (SPVM, 2025). Per-city only — not comparable across cities (different offence definitions).
Income2016 census (Toronto, Vancouver, Edmonton); Calgary is ward-level (2015, 14 wards); Ottawa median household 2020; Montréal 2021 census tracts aggregated.
Low income (LIM-AT)Share below StatCan’s after-tax low-income line — 2021 census tracts, consistent across all six cities.
Census tracts & demographics
Tract variables2021 Census — income, dwelling value, ownership, immigration, visible minority, education, employment, age, population & density (6,052 tracts).
Rent-to-incomeMedian monthly rent (StatCan char. 1480) × 12 ÷ median household income, 2021. Higher = less affordable.
GTA municipalities2021 Census demographics, 55 Greater Toronto municipalities.
